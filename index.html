Haldi

The Haldi ceremony is a pre-wedding ritual in Indian culture, where the bride and groom are smeared with a paste of turmeric, yogurt, and other ingredients. The ceremony is believed to bring good luck, ward off evil, and enhance the beauty and glow of the couple. The Haldi paste is also said to have antiseptic and healing properties, which can help the couple relax and prepare for their big day.
